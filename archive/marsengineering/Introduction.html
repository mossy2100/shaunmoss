Welcome to Mars Engineering.

Mars Engineering is a totally new organisation (September 2001), put together by Mars Society members, with the purpose of earning money to research, develop, and fund Mars missions.

Our basic strategy is to start with the provision of world-class web and software development services, and progress to online games and Mars movies. We have allocated 20% of our profits for Mars missions, and 20% for business development.

Eventually we plan to open our first Earth-based physical location for the purposes of research and development in the fields of internet, virtual reality, artificial intelligence, and robotics.

We will eventually expand into other fields of technology necessary for Mars colonisation, such as spacesuits, life-support systems, recycling, genetic engineering, mining, and advanced materials.

By choosing Mars Engineering for your web or software project, you are helping open up Mars for colonisation and thus benefiting the whole human family.
