Tile Puzzle

  1. To start a new game, choose a world and click "New game".
  2. Click any tile to move it into the adjacent blank space.
  3. The goal is to solve the puzzle in the least number of moves.
  4. Your score starts at 0. Every time you click a tile, it will go up by 1.
  5. If you click a tile that can't move, that still counts towards your score!
